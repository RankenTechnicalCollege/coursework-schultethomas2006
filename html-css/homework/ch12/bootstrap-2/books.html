Meditations

Marcus Aurelius

As someone who takes interest in philosophy, Meditations is a must-read. It was written by the Roman emperor Marcus Aurelius, and serves as a guide on Stoic philosophy and self-improvement.

1984

George Orwell

1984 is a widely known dystopian novel that takes place in the fictional totalitarian super-nation of Oceania, in which private life is totally eliminated and any thoughts or actions out of line with the government are severely punished and erased.

Brave New World

Aldous Huxley

Brave New World is a dystopian novel that takes place in the totalitarian World State and depicts a society that is engineered for stability and happiness through genetic engineering, conditioning, and drug use.
