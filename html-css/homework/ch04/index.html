Benefits of Weightlifting

Weightlifting improves strength, confidence and overall health. It also boosts energy levels and supports a healthy lifestyle.

Essential Gear

Some key items include a lifting belt, proper shoes, and chalk. These help with form, stability, and grip strength.

My Routine

I like to go as often as possible and follow a push/pull/legs routine. On Push day, I do chest, tricep, and shoulder exercises like bench press, incline bench, shoulder press, skull crushers, and tricep push downs. On Pull day, I do back and bicep exercises like bicep curls, back rows, and pull ups or lat pull downs. On Leg day, I do leg presses, leg extensions, hamstring curls, and calf raises.
