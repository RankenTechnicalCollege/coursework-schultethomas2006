Recipe

Frikadelle

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Ground Pork
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Onions
  • Bread Crumbs
  • Eggs
  • (Optional) Choice of seasonings

Overview of How to Make

  1. Mix beef and pork into a large bowl
  2. Add salt, pepper, onions, eggs, bread crumbs and mix by hand
  3. Once sufficiently mixed, take portions of the meat and press into patties
  4. Fry in a skillet until at least 160 degrees Fahrenheit and until they are browned, including the center.
