Albums & Reviews

Parachutes (2000)
This debut album introduced Coldplay’s emotional, melodic sound. Songs like “Yellow” and “Shiver” became instant classics and set the stage for their future success.
A Rush of Blood to the Head (2002)
Considered by many to be their best work, this album features well known tracks like “Clocks” and “The Scientist.” The mix of raw emotion and polished production makes it unforgettable.
Viva La Vida or Death and All His Friends (2008)
This album marked a creative shift, blending orchestral elements with Coldplay’s style. The title track “Viva La Vida” became a global hit.
